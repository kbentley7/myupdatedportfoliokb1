About me

Until recently I was a teacher for 17 years. Prior to earning my BS in Education I was an EMT for 10 years. In additon, for the past 26 years I worked for UPS as a PartTime Operations Supervisor on the midnight shift, a postion that I still currently hold. Earning my degree while working was stressful indeed, however with support from my wife of 29 years, I was able to graduate from America's first HBCU, Cheyney University of PA with Cum Laude honors. As I embark on this new adventure of coding, I will definitely draw from my life experiences to persevere in this exciting endeavor.
